Selecting an Aztec Diamond

While holding down command, mousing over the intersection points of the grid causes an orange circle to pop up, representing a clickable vertex.
An orange diamond represents the squares that will be selected.
Aztec diamond selected!
Note that Aztec rectangles can be selected in the same manner.

Selecting a Rectangle

While holding down shift, click one square. This will be one corner of the rectangle.
Still holding down shift, click the opposite corner. Rectangle selected!
